Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, or order details when you contact us or place an inquiry.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide the services you request (for example, shipping or payment processing if added in the future).

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or order requests
  • Send updates about your order or upcoming events (optional)
  • Improve our products and services

Cookies & Tracking

At this time, Lindin Love does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. If this changes in the future, we will update this page to reflect it.

Data Protection

We take reasonable security measures to safeguard the information you share with us. However, no online transmission is completely secure.

If you have concerns, please contact us directly.
