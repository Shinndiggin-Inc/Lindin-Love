Privacy Policy

Lindin Love Freeze-Dried Foods respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information. This may include:

  • Your name and email address if you contact us directly.
  • Order details for custom or bulk requests.
  • General website traffic data (non-personal).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to messages, questions, or order requests.
  • Prepare custom orders or communicate about your inquiry.
  • Improve our website and services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to complete an order (such as shipping).

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use simple analytics tools to understand visitor traffic. These tools collect non-identifiable information such as pages viewed or browser type.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you share with us. However, no website or email transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request:

  • To review the information we have about you
  • To update or correct information
  • To request deletion of your information

Just contact us by email.
