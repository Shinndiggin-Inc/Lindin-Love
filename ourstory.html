Our Family Journey

How Lindin Love came to life — built with heart, teamwork, and a whole lot of crunch.

Our beginnings

We are a family of five who dreamed of starting our own business.

In 2023, we took a leap of faith and launched Lindin Love Freeze Dried Foods. Along with offering our delicious products to our online followers, we were thrilled to connect with our local community by selling our goods at craft fairs and pop-up shops.

Our very first craft fair took place in November 2023, and it was such a fun experience that we immediately became passionate about it. The most rewarding part of owning our business is working together as a family.

The name "Lindin" is a blend of letters from each of our children's names, while "Love" reflects the love we share for each other and for the products we create. We sincerely hope you’ll join us on the Lindin Love journey.

How the Freeze-Drying Process Works

Our freeze-drying machine is roughly the size of a mini-refrigerator and features a large cylinder that holds several trays of candy or food.

The freeze-drying process occurs inside a vacuum chamber. First, the candy is cooled to a very low temperature, freezing any moisture inside. Then a gentle heating process begins, causing the moisture to evaporate while the sugar expands — often changing texture and enhancing flavor.
