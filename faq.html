These are the most common questions we get at markets, events, and online. You can replace or expand them as your business grows.

How long do freeze-dried foods last?
Properly sealed, most of our products stay fresh 12–18 months. Fruits can last even longer if stored in a cool, dry place.

What is freeze-drying?
Freeze-drying removes water using cold + vacuum instead of heat. This preserves color, flavor, and nutrients better than dehydrating.
Does freeze-dried candy taste different?
Yes — most candies expand, crack open, and become airy with intense flavor. Many customers prefer the freeze-dried versions!
