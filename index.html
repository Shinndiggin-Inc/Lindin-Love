Our story

Rooted in the farm, made with love.

Lindin Love Freeze-Dried Foods started as a way to stretch the harvest a little further — and turned into a way to share those flavors with more families, markets, and events.

From fresh to freeze-dried

Every batch is prepped by hand, laid out on trays, and frozen before we gently pull the moisture out. What stays? Color, aroma, and all that farm-grown flavor.

  • Small-batch runs for quality control
  • Real ingredients you can pronounce
  • Perfect for snacking, topping, and gifting

How it works

Farm to freeze-dryer to you.

Whether you’re grabbing a couple of bags at a local market or planning bulk orders for an event, this is what happens behind the scenes.

Step 1
Pick & prepare
We wash, slice, and prep fruits or candies in small batches, ready for the freeze dryer trays.
Step 2
Freeze & dry
The freeze-drying cycle slowly removes moisture while keeping shape, color, and flavor intact.
Step 3
Seal & label
Each bag is sealed for freshness and labeled with flavor, batch details, and best-by date.
Step 4
Markets & orders
You’ll find us at local markets, pop-ups, and through direct pre-order — with custom options for events.
